    About

    Salamotu Dosumu Skills Acquisition Foundation For Women And Underprivileged, Is A Non-Governmental Organization [NGO] That Functions Independently Of Any Government.
    It Is Ussually A Non-Profit Organization Established On Community, National And International Levels To Serve A Social Or Political Goal Such As A Huanitarian Cause And The Protection Of The Enviroment By Skills Training And Talent Development.
    We Offer Free Skills Acquisition, Women Development, Trainings, Seminars, And Yearly Talent Exibition And Empowerment.
